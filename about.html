All About Me

Professional Me

With over a decade of experience in higher education, I've journeyed from counseling to academic advising, and now I’m diving into the world of instructional design.

My academic adventures include a Bachelor of Science in Psychology and a Master of Arts in Counseling, and I’m currently leveling up with a Master of Science in Learning Technology and Design, which I am projected to complete by May 2025.

As an Instructional Designer I at Missouri University of Science and Technology, I get to team up with faculty to create learning experiences that are engaging and allow students to grow in their knowledge base. This has been a tough transition since I am not naturally creative, but I know I can do it and it is an adventure I am willing to take.
